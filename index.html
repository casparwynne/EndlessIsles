For as long as records existed, the southern sea was blocked by a monstrous storm known only as the Stormwall. Sailors who drifted too close vanished or returned broken, mumbling about flashes of land and strange lights in the clouds.

Ten years ago, the Stormwall collapsed without warning. In its place: an immense archipelago, stretching far beyond the reach of any chart. The first expeditions found islands teeming with unusual creatures, ancient ruins, and untold riches—some of it gold, some of it stranger still.

The nations of the world scrambled to send explorers, colonists, and scholars. The Flotilla was formed soon after to manage disputes and offer a fragile framework for cooperation. Yet ambition, piracy, and mystery still rule these waters.

Where the Isles came from, and why they were hidden for so long, remains unknown. Some whisper of sleeping gods. Others speak of old empires, or magic older than the world itself. Whatever the truth—every island adds a new line to the story.

How to Use the Island Generator

  1. Click “Generate Island” to discover a new isle—its name, type, climate, features, and strange secrets.
  2. Each island generated by the tool is based on a unique seed. This seed controls all the random elements—like island name, climate, features, and mysteries—so the same seed always gives you the same island. You can store the seed in your notes to bring back the same island later.
  3. Use the result as inspiration for a session, encounter, or full adventure. Every island is unique!
  4. Want a new one? Click again! Islands in the Endless Isles are as unpredictable as the sea itself.
  5. Optional: Filter settings (if available) let you adjust island size, threat level, or biome preference.
  6. Save or copy the results for your campaign notes or share them with your crew.

Tip: Add your own twists. The Isles reward creativity—and punish the complacent.
